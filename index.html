Three Distinct Phases

Designed for learning, hypertrophy and strength respectively

Beginner Phase

4 Weeks

Designed to guide total beginners through the journey of fitness. Exercises are chosen for their simplicity, with gradual increase in complexity to help prepare for the more advanced and intricate exercises & workouts.

Hypertrophic Phase

3 Weeks

Focusing solely on building muscle, with the added experience of the first phase we have introduced compound and isolating movements. Each week is formulated to initiate protein synthesis every 48 hours for maximal muscle building.

Strength Phase

3 Weeks

The most complex phase of the program, with the goal of incresing strength, we bring in the concept of RPE and low-rep training. It is not recomended to start this phase if you haven't finished the previous phases, as it is very high intensity.

You can cycle each phase for as long as you like, depending on your goals
